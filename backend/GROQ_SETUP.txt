========================================
GROQ INTEGRATION - ADD TO YOUR .env FILE
========================================

Get your free API key from: https://console.groq.com/keys

Then add these 2 lines to your backend/.env file:

GROQ_API_KEY=your_groq_api_key_here
GROQ_MODEL=llama-3.1-70b-versatile

========================================

PRIMARY MODEL (use this for everything):
✅ llama-3.1-70b-versatile
   - Best quality
   - 128K context window
   - Perfect for chat, voice, memory, tools
   - 50+ tokens/sec

Your Vezora AI will now use models in this order:
